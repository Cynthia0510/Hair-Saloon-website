Our story

“ We have heard people complain bitterly about the services they received at one or more saloons, and also about the stress of asking and enquiring about their services. We have also heard salonist complain about having no customers at all in a day and unable to pay for their shop rents. Our website was created in response to these complaints to bridge the gap between customers and salonist. We provide a ready market for both parties. Our workers are always on time to deliver. ”


Our mission

“ Our mission is to help you look good at your own comfort and relaxation. We have several services for you to choose from. We try to remove the hassle involved searching for a good and dependable hair stylist, makeup artist etc and payment for service. when you can do both from the comfort of your home. ”


Our vision

“ We are looking forward to expanding our business across Nigeria, with improvements in our customer satisfaction and better work policy. ”
